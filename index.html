Who We Are?

Savich18 Games is an independent indie game studio focused on creating memorable gaming experiences.

Our projects are primarily developed by one main author with support from assistants and friends who share our vision.

Here we publish news, updates, and announcements about our games. We believe in transparency and enjoy sharing our development journey with the gaming community.

We specialize in games with unique mechanics, compelling stories, and distinctive visual styles that stand out in the indie gaming scene.

Every project we create is a labor of love, combining innovative gameplay with artistic expression to deliver experiences that resonate with players.
