THE CONCEPT

Protocol Zero is a psychological horror experience that blurs the line between observer and participant. You are hired by the enigmatic Kessler Foundation to document "experimental therapy sessions" through a surveillance system. But as you watch, you realize the subjects' pain isn't just physical—it's being harvested, quantified, and commodified. Your camera isn't just recording; it's an instrument of extraction.

YOUR ROLE

As a remote operator, you control an advanced surveillance network. Monitor multiple subjects simultaneously, switch between camera feeds, and document critical events. The Foundation rewards you for capturing moments of extreme distress—but each decision to record pushes you deeper into ethical oblivion. How much suffering will you document for profit?

FEATURES & EXPERIENCE

Dynamic Surveillance System

Control multiple camera angles, switch between infrared/night vision modes, and manipulate surveillance equipment in real-time.

Bio-Metric Monitoring

Track subjects' vital signs—heart rate, stress levels, pain indicators—and optimize your recording for maximum "extraction value."

Moral Choice System

Every recording decision affects the narrative, character fates, and your own psychological state. Intervention has consequences.
