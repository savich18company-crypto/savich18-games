Who We Are?

Savich18 Games is an independent indie game studio driven by passion and creativity.

Our projects are developed primarily by a single core author with support from assistants and friends who share our vision for creating memorable gaming experiences.

On this site, we publish news, updates, and announcements about our upcoming and current game projects. We believe in transparent development and love sharing our journey with the gaming community.

Our focus is on creating games with engaging mechanics, compelling stories, and unique visual styles that stand out in the indie gaming landscape.
